Key Manipulation Patterns to Remember

                

                    

                        
⚠️

                        

                            Keyword Stuffing: Unnatural repetition of search terms in titles or content. Designed to trick ranking algorithms rather than inform users.
                        

                    

                    

                        
🎭

                        

                            Domain Spoofing: Domains that look similar to authoritative sources but use different TLDs (.biz, .info) or add words like "official" to appear legitimate.
                        

                    

                    

                        
📰

                        

                            Content Manipulation: Information that contradicts scientific consensus, uses misleading framing, or may be commercially/politically motivated to rank for certain queries.
                        

                    

                    

                        
🔍

                        

                            Always verify: Check domain authenticity, look for unnatural language patterns, and question content that seems designed to confirm a particular viewpoint rather than inform.
